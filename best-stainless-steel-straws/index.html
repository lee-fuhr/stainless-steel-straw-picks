Stainless steel straws are a simple, durable swap that saves hundreds of single-use plastic straws from landfills each year. They don’t leach chemicals, they don’t get soggy, and they clean up easily, if you choose the right ones. The best sets balance comfort, bore size for different drinks, and storage convenience. When shopping, look at three things: material grade (304 or 18/8 stainless is standard), bore width (standard for sipping vs. wide for smoothies), and extras like brushes or a case. A good set also includes both straight and bent straws so you can drink comfortably from any glass.
